# Equity Curve Analysis: Performance Visualization

## I. Definition
Plot of account balance over time or trades.

## II. Ideal Characteristics
- Upward sloping
- Low volatility
- Small drawdowns
- Consistent growth

## III. Metrics from Curve
- Maximum drawdown
- Drawdown duration
- Slope (growth rate)
- Volatility of returns

## IV. Equity Curve Trading
Trade the strategy only when its equity curve is above MA:
$$Trade\ if: Equity > MA(Equity)$$

## V. Warning Signs
- Extended flat period
- Increasing drawdowns
- Volatility spike
- Curve breakdown

## VI. Comparison
Compare strategies via equity curve overlay:
- Smoother = Better risk-adjusted
- Steeper = Higher return
