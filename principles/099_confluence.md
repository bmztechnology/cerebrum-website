# Confluence Trading: Multi-Factor Analysis

## I. Definition
Multiple independent factors aligning at same level.

## II. Types of Confluence
- Fibonacci levels
- Moving averages
- Support/resistance
- Pivot points
- Round numbers
- Trendlines

## III. Power of Confluence
$$P(Reaction) \propto \# Confluent\ Factors$$

More factors = Stronger level.

## IV. Example
- 50 EMA
- 61.8% Fibonacci
- Previous structure support
- Round number (1.1000)

= Very strong level.

## V. Trading Application
Prioritize setups with 3+ confluence factors.
Avoid single-factor trades.

## VI. Stop Placement
Place stop where confluence invalidates.
