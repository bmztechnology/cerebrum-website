# Range Trading: Sideways Market Strategy

## I. Definition
Trading within horizontal bounds when market lacks trend.

## II. Identification
- ADX < 20
- Price oscillating between levels
- No higher highs/higher lows pattern

## III. Strategy
- Buy at support
- Sell at resistance
- Stop beyond range boundaries
- Target opposite boundary

## IV. Tools
- Stochastic oscillator
- RSI
- Bollinger Bands
- Range-based indicators

## V. Entry Confirmation
Wait for reversal signal at boundary:
- Rejection candle
- Oversold/overbought oscillator
- Volume decrease

## VI. Exit Range Mode
Stop trading range when:
- ADX rises above 25
- Clear breakout with volume
- New higher high or lower low
