# Eurodollar System: The Shadow Money

## I. Executive Summary

A **Eurodollar** is simply a US Dollar deposit held in a bank outside the United States. It is unregulated by the Federal Reserve (No reserve requirements, no FDIC). This offshore system creates money through credit expansion unchecked by the Fed. It is the true lubricant of Global Trade, and when it dries up, the world experiences a "Dollar Shortage."

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 The Dollar Smile
When Global Growth is strong $\to$ Borrow Eurodollars to invest (Dollar sells off).
When Global Crisis hits $\to$ Repay Eurodollar debt (Scramble for Dollars).
Result: USD rises in a crash.
The Eurodollar Short Squeeze.

### 3.2 LIBOR (London Interbank Offered Rate)
The price of Eurodollars.
Now replaced by SOFR (Domestic Repo).
The transition kills the "Credit Sensitivity" signal.
Eurodollars relied on "Trust" between banks.

## IV. Strategic Applications

### 4.1 DXY Prediction
DXY is heavily influenced by the Eurodollar funding stress.
If "Cross Currency Basis" is negative (Dollar shortage), DXY will rally even if US Data is bad.
The world *needs* dollars to service $13T of debt.

### 4.2 Emerging Markets (EM)
EM Corps borrow in Eurodollars (lower rates).
Earn revenue in Local Currency (BRL, TRY).
If USD rises, Debt increases, Revenue (in USD terms) falls.
"Original Sin."
Short EM Equities when US Dollar strengthens.
