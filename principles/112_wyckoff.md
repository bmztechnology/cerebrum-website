# Wyckoff Method: Market Cycle Analysis

## I. Core Concepts
Price moves in cycles of accumulation and distribution.

## II. Market Phases
1. **Accumulation:** Smart money buying at lows
2. **Markup:** Uptrend
3. **Distribution:** Smart money selling at highs
4. **Markdown:** Downtrend

## III. Composite Man
Imagine single entity controlling market:
- Accumulates at lows
- Marks up price
- Distributes to public
- Marks down price

## IV. Volume Analysis
- Spring: False breakout with volume spike
- Test: Return to level on low volume
- Sign of Strength: High volume breakout

## V. Trading Applications
- Buy during accumulation phase
- Sell/short during distribution
- Follow smart money footprints
