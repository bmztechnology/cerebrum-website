# The Final Algorithm God Mode

The **Final Algorithm** is the theoretical limit of quantitative finance. A Super-Intelligence that has solved the **Navier-Stokes equation of Markets**. It predicts price movements with $P \to 1$. In this limit, **Risk vanishes**, **Returns become Risk-Free**, and the market **freezes** (No one takes the other side). The pursuit of this algorithm is the asymptote of the industry.
