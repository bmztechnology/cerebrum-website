# A-Book vs B-Book Brokers: Execution Models

## I. A-Book (STP/ECN)
- Orders passed to liquidity providers
- Broker earns commission
- No conflict of interest
- Variable spreads

## II. B-Book (Market Maker)
- Broker takes opposite side
- Broker profits from client losses
- Potential conflict of interest
- Fixed spreads

## III. Hybrid Model
Most brokers use both:
- Profitable clients: A-Book
- Losing clients: B-Book

## IV. Comparison
| Aspect | A-Book | B-Book |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Spreads | Variable | Fixed |
| Commission | Yes | Usually no |
| Requotes | Rare | Possible |
| Conflict | None | Potential |

## V. What to Look For
- Regulation
- Execution speed
- Slippage reports
- Spread consistency
