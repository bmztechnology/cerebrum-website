# The Master Algorithm: Synthesis of 100 Principles

## I. Executive Summary

This final chapter integrates the preceding 99 principles into a unified "Master Algorithm." It demonstrates how the disparate concepts—Mathematics, Psychology, Structure, Order Flow—coalesce into a single decision-making engine. The Master Algorithm is the realization of the "Cerebrum" vision: a holistic, fractal, and robust trading methodology.

## II. The Process Flow (The Engine)

### Phase 1: Context (Macro & Intermarket)
*Inputs:* Central Bank Policy (058), Intermarket Analysis (096), Seasonality (040).
-   **Check:** Is the USD fundamentally strong? (Yields rising?).
-   **Filter:** Is today a Tier 1 News Day? (080).
-   *Output:* **Directional Bias** (Bull/Bear/Neutral).

### Phase 2: Structure (The Map)
*Inputs:* Market Structure (061), Major S/R, Supply/Demand (022).
-   **Check:** Where are we in the Wyckoff Cycle (093)?
-   **Check:** Are we at a Key Monthly/Weekly Level?
-   *Output:* **Zone of Interest** (POI).

### Phase 3: Setup (The Trigger)
*Inputs:* Chart Patterns (038), Candlesticks (064), Confluence, Indicators (081-085).
-   **Check:** Price approaches POI.
-   **Check:** Indicator Divergence (078) signals exhaustion.
-   **Check:** Lower Timeframe Structure Shift (CHoCH).
-   **Check:** Volume Profile (090) rejection.
-   *Output:* **Entry Signal**.

### Phase 4: Risk (The Shield)
*Inputs:* Volatility (089), Kelly Criterion (065), Risk of Ruin (098).
-   **Calculate:** Stop Loss based on Structure + ATR buffer.
-   **Calculate:** Position Size based on 1% Risk cap.
-   **Check:** Portfolio Heat (076).
-   *Output:* **Order Execution**.

### Phase 5: Management (The Pilot)
*Inputs:* Trade Management (073), Scaling (071), Psychology (051).
-   **Action:** Scale out 50% at TP1.
-   **Action:** Move Stop to Breakeven (070).
-   **Action:** Trail Stop using Parabolic SAR (088) or Structure.
-   *Output:* **Exit**.

### Phase 6: Review (The Feedback)
*Inputs:* Journaling (052), KPIs (097).
-   **Action:** Log the trade.
-   **Action:** Analyze MAE/MFE (057).
-   *Output:* **System Refinement**.
