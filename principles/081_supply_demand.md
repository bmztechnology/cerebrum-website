# Supply and Demand Zones: Institutional Levels

## I. Definition
Zones where institutional orders created imbalance.

## II. Supply Zone
Area where selling overwhelmed buying:
- Price dropped sharply from level
- Return to zone = Selling opportunity

## III. Demand Zone
Area where buying overwhelmed selling:
- Price rose sharply from level
- Return to zone = Buying opportunity

## IV. Zone Identification
1. Find explosive move (strong impulse)
2. Mark base before move
3. Zone = Base area

## V. Trading Rules
- Enter at zone
- Stop beyond zone
- Target opposite zone

## VI. Fresh vs Tested
- Fresh zone: Never tested = Stronger
- Tested zone: Touched and held = Still valid
- Broken zone: Violated = No longer valid
