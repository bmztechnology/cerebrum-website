# Generative AI for Alpha GPT in Finance

## I. Executive Summary

**Generative AI** (LLMs, Diffusion Models) moves beyond classification to **Creation**. In finance, this means **Synthetic Data Generation**, **Code Generation** (Quants becoming Architects), and **Automated Research**. The synthesis warns that while productivity explodes, the **Barrage of Noise** (AI-generated news/spam) makes **Truth** the scarcest commodity.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (Synthetic Time Series)
GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks).
Generator creates fake price path. Discriminator guesses if real or fake.
Result: Infinite "realistic" market history.
Train RL agents on 10,000 years of synthetic data.
Covering "Black Swans" that haven't happened yet.

### Definition 2.2 (Copilot Coding)
Quant Developer writes comments.
AI writes the C++/Python code.
Productivity $\times$ 10.
Barrier to entry for algorithmic trading drops.
"Script Kiddies" of finance.
Risk: Subtle bugs in AI code that no one reads.

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 The Knowledge Work Collapse
Junior Analyst job: Read 100 reports, summarize in 1 page.
GenAI does this in 10 seconds.
The "Apprentice Model" of Wall Street breaks.
How do Juniors learn to be Seniors?
Structural shift in Investment Banking hierarchy.

### 3.2 Epistemic Pollution
Internet flooded with AI content.
Sentiments models trained on internet data ingest AI trash.
Model Collapse (AI training on AI output).
Primacy of "Orginal Source" data (Exchange feeds, verified filings).
Bloomberg Terminal premium increases (Walled Garden of Truth).
