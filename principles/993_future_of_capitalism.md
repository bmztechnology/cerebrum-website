# The Future of Capitalism

## I. Executive Summary

**Capitalism** is the best system we've found for allocating scarce resources, but it is facing **Existential Crises** (Inequality, Climate, Artificial Abundance). The synthesis predicts a mutation towards **"Stakeholder Capitalism"** or **"Techno-Feudalism."** As AI drives the cost of labor to zero, the link between **Work** and **Income** breaks, requiring a fundamental rewrite of the Social Contract.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (Techno-Feudalism - Varoufakis)
Big Tech platforms are the new Fiefdoms.
We are the Serfs (providing data for free).
We pay rent (App Store fees / Attention) to the Lords (Bezos/Musk).
Markets are replaced by Algorithms.
Capitalism (Profit via competition) $\to$ Feudalism (Rent extraction via monopoly).

### Definition 2.2 (Post-Scarcity Economics)
Star Trek.
Replicator (3D Printing / AI) makes goods free.
Marginal Cost $\to$ 0.
Capitalism relies on Scarcity/Price signals.
Without scarcity, Capitalism dissolves.
Evolution into a "Resource Based Economy" or "Reputation Economy"?

## III. Theoretical Framework

Decentralized Autonomous Organizations.
Replacing the Corporation (Hierarchical) with the Network (Flat).

Programmatic Governance.
Hyper-Capitalism? Or Marxist Collectivism?
Paradoxically, it's both.
