# The End of the Dollar Hegemony

## I. Executive Summary

The **US Dollar** has reigned since Bretton Woods (1944). It enjoys "Exorbitant Privilege" (Cheap borrowing) and weaponizes sanctions. The synthesis argues that the **Weaponization of the Financial System** (freezing Russia's reserves) creates a **Game Theoretic Incentive** for rivals (China, Global South) to build alternative rails. The future is likely **Multipolar**.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (Network Effects in Currency)
1. Unit of Account (Pricing Oil).
2. Medium of Exchange (SWIFT).
3. Store of Value (T-Bills).
USD dominates all three.
Network switching costs are high.
You need a "Killer App" to displace the Dollar. (Gold? Yuan? Bitcoin?).

### Definition 2.2 (The Petrodollar)
Deal (1970s): US protects Saudi Arabia. Saudi prices Oil in USD.
Global demand for Oil = Global demand for Dollars.
Recycles petrodollars into Treasuries.
Trend: "Petroyuan." Saudi accepting CNY for Oil.
Gradual erosion of the peg.

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 Triffin Dilemma in Reverse
If US sanctions everyone, World stops hoarding Dollars.
US runs smaller deficits?
Or US Yields spike (Loss of foreign buyers)?
The "Exorbitant Burden."
Domestic inflation vs Global power trade-off.

### 3.2 The BRICS+ Currency
Commodity-backed unit?
Gold backing?
Problem: Requires Trust and Rule of Law (which BRICS lack).
More likely: A "Trade Settlement Unit" (like SDR) rather than a Retail Currency.
Fragmentation of global trade into blocs.
