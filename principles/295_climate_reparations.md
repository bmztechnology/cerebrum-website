# Climate Reparations: The North-South Divide

## I. Executive Summary

**Climate Reparations** (Loss and Damage Fund) argues that the Global North (Historical Emitters) owes the Global South (Victims) for climate change damages. Strategically, this is evolving into financial transfers and **Carbon Protectionism**. Mechanisms like the EU's **CBAM (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism)** act as green tariffs, forcing the South to decarbonize or lose market access.
