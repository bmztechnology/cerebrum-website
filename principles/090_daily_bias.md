# Daily Bias: Directional Expectation

## I. Definition
Expected market direction for the day based on analysis.

## II. Determining Bias
- Higher timeframe trend
- Previous day close location
- Key levels above/below
- Institutional activity

## III. Types
- **Bullish bias:** Expect up day
- **Bearish bias:** Expect down day
- **Neutral:** Wait for direction

## IV. Trading with Bias
Only take setups aligned with daily bias.
Counter-bias trades = Higher risk.

## V. Invalidation
Bias invalid when key structure breaks.
Flip or step aside.
