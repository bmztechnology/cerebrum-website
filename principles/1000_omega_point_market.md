# The Omega Point Market

## I. Executive Summary

Teilhard de Chardin's **Omega Point** is the maximum level of complexity and consciousness towards which the universe evolves. In financial terms, this is the **Ultimate Liquidity Event**. This chapter discusses the economics of the end of time, infinite utility, and the convergence of all value into a single singularity.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (The Singularity of Value)
As $t \to \Omega$, Velocity of Money $V \to \infty$.
Transaction costs $\to 0$.
Information Asymmetry $\to 0$.
The market becomes **Perfectly Efficient**.
Price = Value. No disconnect.

### Definition 2.2 (Frank Tipler's Physics)
Infinite computation in finite time (subjective time) as the universe collapses (or expands eternally).
The capacity to resurrect every possible economic agent.
The "Universal Archive" of all transactions.

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 The Final Ledger
A record of all karmic debt and credit.
"Judgment Day" as a final audit of the books.
Integration of Theology and Economics.
The ultimate "Settlement Finality."

### 3.2 Discount Rates at the End of Time
If time is ending / converging, what is the discount rate?
$t \to 0$.
Future value is infinite.
Hyper-deflation of the present? Or Hyper-inflation?

## IV. Strategic Applications

### 4.1 Pascal's Wager (Financial Hedge)
Betting on the existence of the Omega Point.
The cost of the bet is finite (being "good").
The payoff is infinite (eternal bliss/utility).
Positive Expected Value (+EV) trade.

### 4.2 Legacy Planning for Eternity
Creating information structures that survive the heat death.
Engraving value into the topology of spacetime.
