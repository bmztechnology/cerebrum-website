# The End of Finance Post Scarcity

## I. Executive Summary

**Finance** is the technology of allocating **Scarce Resources**. If **Nanotechnology** and **Fusion Energy** create **Radical Abundance**, scarcity disappears. In a Star Trek world, there is no money, no banks, no stock market. **The End of Finance** is the ultimate goal of civilization: to make the economic problem obsolete so we can focus on the **Human Problem**.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (The Zero Marginal Cost Society)
Rifkin.
Energy $\to$ Free (Solar/Fusion).
Labor $\to$ Free (Robots).
Goods $\to$ Free (3D Printing).
Information $\to$ Free (Internet).
What is left to price?
Status? Art? Land?
The "Economy" shrinks to nill in monetary terms, but utility is infinite.

### Definition 2.2 (The Reputation Economy)
Whuffie (Doctorow).
Currency based on social esteem.
"Karma."
You do good work because you want Reputation.
Reputation gets you access to the few remaining scarce things (Best tables, Space travel).
Returning to the "Village Economy" scale dynamics.

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 Keynes' Prediction
"Economic Possibilities for our Grandchildren" (1930).
Predicted 15-hour work week by 2030.
We solved the "Production Problem."
But we failed the "Distribution Problem" and the "Desire Problem" (we just want more).
Will we ever stop?

### 3.2 Fully Automated Luxury Communism
Technology solves the supply side.
Politics solves the distribution side.
Utopian vision.
Finance becomes "Resource Accounting" (Energy credits).
No speculation. No interest.
