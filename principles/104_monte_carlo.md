# Monte Carlo Simulation: Strategy Robustness

## I. Purpose
Test strategy under randomized conditions.

## II. Method
1. Take actual trades
2. Randomize order
3. Run multiple simulations
4. Analyze distribution

## III. What It Shows
- Best case equity curve
- Worst case drawdown
- 95th percentile outcomes
- Strategy robustness

## IV. Interpretation
If worst case acceptable → Strategy viable.
If edge disappears → Strategy fragile.

## V. Applications
- Position sizing validation
- Drawdown estimation
- Risk management testing

## VI. Limitations
- Assumes trade independence
- Can't account for regime change
- Past may not predict future
