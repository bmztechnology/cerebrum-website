# Multipolarity Thesis: The End of Pax Americana

## I. Executive Summary

**Multipolarity** implies a world with 3+ centers of power (US, China, EU/India?), replacing the Unipolar moment (1991-2020) of US Hegemony. Historically, multipolar systems are unstable and prone to conflict (e.g., pre-WWI Europe). Economically, it means the fragmenting of the Dollar System, the rise of **BRICS+**, and the weaponization of interdependence.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (Kindleberger Trap)
When the rising power (China) is unwilling to lead, and the declining power (US) is unable to lead.
Global Public Goods (Security, Shipping Lanes, Reserve Currency) go under-provided.
Result: Chaos / Vacuum.
1930s was a Kindleberger Trap (UK down, US isolationist).

### Definition 2.2 (Splinternet of Finance)
-   **System A:** SWIFT / CHIPS / Fedwire. (US Control).
-   **System B:** CIPS / SPFS / mBridge. (China/Russia Control).
-   **System C:** Crypto / DeFi. (Neutral).
Multipolarity forces nations to chose a stack or bridge multiple.

## III. Theoretical Framework
