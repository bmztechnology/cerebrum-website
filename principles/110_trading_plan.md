# Trading Plan Template: Complete Framework

## I. Overview
Written document defining all trading activities.

## II. Essential Components

### 1. Goals
- Monthly return target
- Maximum drawdown tolerance
- Risk per trade

### 2. Markets
- Currency pairs to trade
- Sessions to trade
- Pairs to avoid

### 3. Strategy Rules
- Entry criteria (specific)
- Exit criteria (stop-loss, take-profit)
- Position sizing formula
- Maximum positions

### 4. Risk Management
- Max risk per trade: ___%
- Max daily risk: ___%
- Max weekly risk: ___%
- Drawdown limits

### 5. Daily Routine
- Pre-market analysis time
- Trading hours
- End-of-day review

### 6. Rules for Self
- After X losses: Stop for day
- After target reached: Consider stopping
- Never break risk rules

## III. Purpose
- Removes emotion
- Ensures consistency
- Enables improvement tracking

## IV. Review Schedule
- Daily: Did I follow the plan?
- Weekly: Adjust minor items
- Monthly: Major evaluation

## References
- Van Tharp, R. (2007). Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom
- Douglas, M. (2000). Trading in the Zone
