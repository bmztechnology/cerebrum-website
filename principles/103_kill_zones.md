# Kill Zones: Optimal Trading Windows

## I. Definition
Time periods with highest probability setups.

## II. Main Kill Zones (EST)
| Session | Time (EST) |
|---------|------------|
| Asian | 20:00-00:00 |
| London | 02:00-05:00 |
| NY | 07:00-10:00 |
| London Close | 10:00-12:00 |

## III. Why Kill Zones Work
- Institutional activity peaks
- Liquidity available
- Trends initiate

## IV. Best Pairs per Zone
- Asian: JPY pairs
- London: EUR, GBP pairs
- NY: USD pairs

## V. Strategy
Focus trading within kill zones.
Other times = Lower probability.

## VI. Avoid
- Low volume hours
- Between sessions
- Friday afternoons
