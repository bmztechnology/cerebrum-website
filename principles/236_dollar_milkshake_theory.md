# Dollar Milkshake Theory: The Liquidity Vacuum

## I. Executive Summary

**The Dollar Milkshake Theory** (Brent Johnson) posits that contrary to "Dollar Collapse" theories, the USD will experience a massive, destructive rally. Reason: The Fed is tightening (sucking the straw) while global central banks are still loose. Since the world owes $13T+ in Dollar-denominated debt, this creates a **Short Squeeze** on a planetary scale. Liquidity (Milkshake) flows from the Periphery (Europe/EM) intto the Core (USA).

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (The Construct)
-   **The Milkshake:** Global Liquidity (Capital).
-   **The Straw:** High US Rates + QT.
-   **The Dynamic:** US sucks capital from the rest of the world.
Result: US Assets (Stocks/Bonds) outperform; Rest of World collapses; USD skyrockets.

### Definition 2.2 (Sovereign Debt Crisis)
Strong USD increases the real burden of foreign dollar debt.
Defaults in Sri Lanka, Pakistan, Egypt.
Flight to Safety $\to$ More USD buying.
Reflexive Loop.

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 Relative Yields vs Absolute Supply
Even if Fed prints money, if ECB/BOJ print *more*, USD strengthens.
USD is the "Cleanest Shirt in the Dirty Laundry."
Higher Yields attracting capital $\to$ USD Up $\to$ US Asset Prices Up.

### 3.2 The End Game
USD rises until it breaks the system.
Fed forced to Pivot and print to save the world (Swap Lines).
But until that break, the trend is UP.

## IV. Strategic Applications
