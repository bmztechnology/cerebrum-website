# The Efficient Market as God

## I. Executive Summary

The **Efficient Market Hypothesis (EMH)** posits that "The Market knows all." This is functionally equivalent to a **Theology**. The Market is **Omniscient** (Prices in all info), **Omnipotent** (Capital allocation determines destiny), and **Inscrutable** (Random Walk). The synthesis explores the quasi-religious faith we place in Price Discovery and the heretics (Behaviorists) who question it.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (The Hayekian Processor)
Hayek: "The Use of Knowledge in Society."
No central planner can know everything.
Prices condense millions of local facts into a single signal.
The Market is a Distributed Computer.
Socialism failed because it lacked this computer (Calculation Problem).

### Definition 2.2 (The Invisible Hand)
Adam Smith.
Providence.
Self-interest leads to social good.
Faith in the system's emergent benevolence.
Crypto "Invisible Hand": Proof of Work + Greed = Secure Network.

## III. Theoretical Framework

### 3.1 Prediction Markets (The Delphic Oracle)
Polymarket / Augur.
"Who will win the election?"
Market is more accurate than polls.
We ask the Market to predict the future.
"Truth is what the market clears at."

### 3.2 Market Fundamentalism
Belief that *all* problems should be solved by markets.
Carbon Credits (Market for Pollution).
School Vouchers (Market for Education).
Dating Apps (Market for Love).
Critique (Sandel): "What Money Can't Buy."
Moral limits of the market.
