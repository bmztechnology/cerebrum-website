# Imbalance: Price Inefficiency Zones

## I. Definition
Area where one-sided trading created price gaps.

## II. Types
- Single candle imbalance
- Multi-candle FVG
- Institutional candle (large body, small wicks)

## III. Trading Logic
Price tends to return to fill imbalances.
Act as support/resistance.

## IV. Identification
Look for:
- Rapid price movement
- Little overlap between candles
- Big body, small wick candles

## V. Strategy
When price revisits imbalance:
- Look for rejection
- Entry with structure break
- Stop beyond imbalance

## VI. Priority
Fresher imbalances > Older imbalances.
