# The Future of Behavioral Finance Biometrics

## I. Executive Summary

The future of Behavioral Finance lies in **Biometrics** and **Big Data**. Moving beyond surveys and lab experiments, researchers now use **Wearables** (Apple Watch, Oura) to track real-time physiological states (HRV, Cortisol, Sleep) of traders. Coupled with **AI** analyzing "Digital Exhaust" (Mouse movements, Social Media sentiment), we are entering the era of **Quantified Behavior**.
