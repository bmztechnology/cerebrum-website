# The Ultimate Algorithm RenTec and Solving the Market

## I. Executive Summary

The quest for the **Ultimate Algorithm** is the search for a machine that extracts Alpha consistently, uncorrelated to the market, with Infinite Capacity. **Renaissance Technologies (Medallion Fund)** represents the pinnacle: 66% annualized returns (gross) for 30 years. The secret is not one "God Algo," but a **Mosaic** of weak signals, **Cost Optimization**, and **Infrastructure Superiority**.

## II. Formal Definitions

### Definition 2.1 (The Fundamental Law of Active Management)
$$ IR = IC \times \sqrt{BR} $$
**IR:** Information Ratio (Risk adjusted return).
**IC:** Information Coefficient (Skill/Correlation).
**BR:** Breadth (Number of independent bets).
RenTec strategy: Low IC (51% win rate), Massive BR (Millions of trades).
Casino logic.

### Definition 2.2 (Capacity Constraints)
As AUM grows, Market Impact grows.
Return decays.
Medallion is capped at \$10B (Returns capital to employees).
The Ultimate Algo knows its own size limit.
