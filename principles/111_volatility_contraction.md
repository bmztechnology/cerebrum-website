# Volatility Contraction Pattern: Breakout Setup

## I. Definition
Decreasing volatility followed by expansion.

## II. Identification
- Narrowing Bollinger Bands
- Decreasing ATR
- Inside bars / compressed candles
- Triangle patterns

## III. Trading Logic
Low volatility precedes high volatility.
Breakout from contraction = High momentum move.

## IV. Entry Method
1. Wait for clear contraction
2. Enter on first expansion candle
3. Direction: Higher TF bias or breakout direction

## V. Stop Placement
Opposite side of contraction range.

## VI. Targets
- Previous swing high/low
- ATR-based projection
- Fibonacci extensions
