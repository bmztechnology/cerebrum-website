# Quick Start

This guide will get you up and running in less than 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Connect to MT5

On launch, Cerebrum automatically attempts to connect to MetaTrader 5.

- ‚úÖ **Connection successful**: The indicator in the top right turns green
- ‚ùå **Failed**: Verify that MT5 is open and configured

## Step 2: Data Extraction

Before you can train models or make predictions, you need to extract historical data.

1. Go to the **Extraction** tab
2. Configure the start year (default: 2015)
3. Click **Smart Extract**

```{note}
The first extraction may take several minutes depending on the amount of data.
```

## Step 3: Model Training

Once data is extracted:

1. Go to the **Training** tab
2. Click **Train All** to train all models
3. Monitor progress in the indicators

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| XGBoost | Fast gradient boosting model |
| LightGBM | Optimized for large datasets |
| RandomForest | Ensemble of decision trees |
| Stacking | Meta-model combining the others |

## Step 4: Predictions

After training:

1. Go to the **Prediction** tab
2. Select a timeframe (e.g., 1h)
3. Click **Predict** or enable automatic predictions
